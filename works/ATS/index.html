Designing a unique taxi station for city of Ankara was the abjective of this small project. I decided to use a modular system which could get formed according to different urban needs. It could become a ATM containing shelter, bus station, WC etc. as long as being able to become taxi station. In modular system a few components have been designed (plates and profiles), yet for the space frame, due to long spans and cantilevers, rods of carbon fiber has been offered.
